Interesting facts about chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the Iguana suborder.
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail taht they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting facts about pandas

  1. Giant pandas (often referred to as simply “pandas”) are black and white bears.
  2. These magnificent mammals are omnivores.
  3. The giant panda’s scientific name is Ailuropoda melanoleuca, which means “black and white cat-foot”.

Interesting facts about teddy bears

  1. The Teddy Bears’ Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step.
  2. Winnie the Pooh was based on a real bear.
  3. The story of Goldilocks And The Three Bears was written in 1837 by Robert Southey who was the Poet Laureate at the time.

Interesting facts about pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intellegent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their incredible navigation skills.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting facts about camels

  1. Camels are a mammal of the Camelidae family.
  2. They are easily distinguished because of their humps.
  3. Camels have been used as a method of transportation for humans for a very long time.

Interesting facts about cicadas

  1. Cicadas are the loudest insects.
  2. Adult cicadas have thick bodies and wings.
  3. There are almost 2000 different species of cicadas in the world, found everywhere except Antarctica.
